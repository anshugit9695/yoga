What we do

Our Treatment Process

At Shanti Ayurvedic Retreat, we follow a step-by-step healing approach that is personalized, natural, and deeply effective. Here’s how our treatment process works

Panchkarma

A deep detoxification therapy that removes toxins from the body through specialized treatments like Vamana, Virechana, Basti, Nasya, and more. It helps reset your internal systems for long-term healing and rejuvenation.

Naturopathy

A natural approach to healing that focuses on diet, fasting, hydrotherapy, mud therapy, and lifestyle correction. It helps the body heal itself by restoring balance through the power of nature

Acupuncture

A traditional Chinese therapy using fine needles at specific points to stimulate nerves and energy flow. It’s effective for pain relief, stress, joint disorders, and hormonal balance

Acupressure

A non-invasive therapy where specific pressure points on the body are stimulated using fingers or tools. It improves blood circulation, reduces stiffness, and supports overall healing.

Herbal Steam Bath

A detox treatment using herbal-infused steam that opens pores, relaxes muscles, and eliminates toxins through sweat. It also improves skin health and respiratory function.

Ayurvedic Massage

A full-body therapeutic oil massage that nourishes tissues, improves circulation, reduces stress, and strengthens the immune system. Each massage is tailored to your dosha (body constitution).
